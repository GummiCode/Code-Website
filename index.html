What is Lo-Fi?

Lo-Fi is a style and an aesthetic used in music, video, fashion and other media. It is characterised by serenity, gentleness, human warmth and androgyny, and often explores the everyday human experience.

Lo-Fi can be many things.

  • It can be a serene trainride.

  • It can be a slice of life.

  • It can be a warm home.

  • It can be retrofuturism.

  • It can be cats. Cats are extremely lo-fi.

Lo-Fi Subjectivity

Lo-Fi is a state of mind. It is therefore subjective. For each person it is different. However common trends in what people consider lo-fi can be determined using modern research methods such as asking strangers and mass-distribution of questionaires to email lists obtained from websites with poor privacy protocols.

Here are fifteen things that our research shows are commonly believed to be lo-fi.

  1. calm
  2. gentleness
  3. comfort
  4. serenity
  5. self-love
  6. loving friends
  7. holding hands
  8. soft sunshine
  9. the smell of the forest
  10. walking on concrete
  11. napping on the couch in a white walled apartment, leafy trees filtering the sunlight through the window
  12. chiffon cake
  13. a hoodie that is perfectly too large
  14. lowercase text
  15. how did you get my email address? please don't write to me again.
