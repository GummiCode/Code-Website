About Me

I've talked to friends who work in the field, I've read about the industry, I've attended workshops, I've completed online courses. The technology is enjoyable to work with, the field is blooming and opportunities are abundant. It's very exciting and I want to be a part of it.

I'm allowing myself 6 months to acquire the skills I need for my first development position including contemporary languages, test-driven development and experience of collaborative projects.
